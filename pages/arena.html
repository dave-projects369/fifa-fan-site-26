Never Known Stories

Behind the Scenes

2010

The Vuvuzela War

The plastic horn that divided the world. Some called it noise pollution. South Africans called it culture. FIFA almost banned it — then didn't. The sound became the anthem of an entire continent.

1994

The Final With No Goals

Brazil vs Italy. The first World Cup Final decided entirely on penalties. 120 minutes of football produced zero goals. Roberto Baggio's missed penalty became one of sport's most iconic images.

1998

Ronaldo's Mystery Illness

Brazil's superstar had a seizure the night before the final. He was pulled from the team sheet — then added back. Brazil lost 3-0 to France. What really happened that night remains disputed.
